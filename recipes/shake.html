Raspberry-Strawberry oatmeal smoothie

A thick, super creamy and filling Strawberry-Raspberry Oatmeal Cheesecake Smoothie
that tastes exactly like a cheesecake….in a glass….for breakfast!

Ingredients and Prep

Prep Time

Duration: Around 15 Mins

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1/2 cup frozen sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 plain , non fat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons quick oats
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Steps

Duration: Around 6 Mins
  1. Blend all ingredients together until smooth. If you find it too thick,
    add a little more milk and blend until done to your liking!
    Top with extra frozen raspberries.
